Executive Summary

ABN Group is a prominent construction, property, and finance company in Australia, operating multiple businesses across Western Australia and Victoria. The company specializes in residential and commercial construction, land development, property investment, and financial services. ABN Group is dedicated to supporting the community, quality apprentice training, sustainable development, and affordable housing. Despite industry challenges, the company has reported a significant financial turnaround, demonstrating resilience and strategic focus. With a comprehensive approach to property development and construction, a commitment to innovation and growth, and a strong emphasis on supporting clients and the community, ABN Group stands out as a leader in the Australian building industry. Key appointments, social initiatives, and successful projects further highlight the company's commitment to excellence and positive impact on society.

Business Overview

The business operates in the Marketing Sales Development & Construction industry, focusing on project management, design and architecture, construction, and sales. The company manages projects including location, budget, timeline, and objectives, along with tasks, milestones, and team members. They also handle design and architecture, material specifications, permits and compliance, budgeting and costing, invoicing and billing, resource management, contractor and subcontractor management, stakeholder communication, safety, quality assurance, supply chain, and site management. Additionally, the business engages in sales and marketing activities, customer segmentation, campaign management, lead generation, social media marketing, SEO, email marketing, affiliate marketing, event marketing, brand management, market research, analytics, reporting, and financial management including accounts, payroll, risk management, and budgeting. The business also manages HR functions, infrastructure and hardware, software applications, cybersecurity, data management, cloud services, DevOps, networking, IT service management, vendor management, disaster recovery, and innovation. Overall, the business is involved in a wide range of activities across various departments to ensure successful project delivery and sales growth.
